<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/check-squares2421/1">Check for Sum of Squares</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Basic</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Given an integer <strong>n</strong>, determine whether there exist two whole numbers a and b such that: <strong>a<sup>2 </sup>+ b<sup>2</sup> = n</strong>. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples :</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">n<strong> = </strong>1</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Output:</strong> true</span><br><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">1<sup>2</sup> + 0<sup>2</sup> = 1. So, the output is true.</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input:</strong> </span><span style="font-size: 18px;">n<strong> = </strong>2</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Output: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">true</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">1<sup>2</sup> + 1<sup>2</sup> = 2. So, the output is true.</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input:</strong> </span><span style="font-size: 18px;">n<strong> = </strong>7</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Output: </strong>false</span> <br><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">We don't have a or b, which when summed after squaring gives 7. So, Output is false.</span></pre>
